Definition of Web App for Managing Capstone Projects (Bachelor's and Master's) for the Computer Science Department:

This platforme designed to manage capstone projects for Bachelor's and Master's students in the Computer Science Department ,This application enables teachers and administrators to effectively manage capstone projects by assigning topics to students based on a specified ranking while adhering to the deadlines set by teachers for theme submissions. Meanwhile, students are required to adhere to the deadlines for selecting the proposed topics.

Instructions for Students

Select choice

A student must choose an exetrenal or internal theme, this facilitates the Management of themes for administration.

Carefully read the themes

A student must read currefuly the themes in case he has chosen an internal themes , as students to choose the themes according to their capacity.

Fill out the form

The student must fill out the form with his information correctly in case he has chosen the internal theme, or exetrenal theme.
